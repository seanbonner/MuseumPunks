In early 2023, the anonymous collector Cozomo de' Medici donated 22 works to LACMA, in would would be the first and largest private collection of blockchain artworks to enter an American art museum. Alongside his famous CryptoPunk #3831 (which he still uses as his identity today) he donated works by 12 other artists including Dmitri Cherniak, Matt DesLauriers, and Monica Rizzoli.
