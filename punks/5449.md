In early 2018 Germany's ZKM was the first major art institution to acquire CryptoPunks for it's collection. Punks # [2554](https://museumpunks.com/2554/), # [1286](https://museumpunks.com/1286/), # [5449](https://museumpunks.com/5449/), and # [2838](https://museumpunks.com/2838/) were purchased by museum employee Daniel Heiss. Unfortunately in 2021 they also became the first (and so far only) major art institution to burn a punk, as # [5449](https://burnedpunks.com/5449/) and # [2838](https://burnedpunks.com/2838/) were sent back to the CryptoPunks contract thanks to copy/paste error while moving them. In 2025 all 4 punks were displayed in the museum as part of their exhibition [*The Story That Never Ends*](https://hub.cryptopunks.app/the-story-that-never-ends).
